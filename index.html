Featured Trip

5 Days Skardu Tour

Our most popular package — by air from Islamabad

Day 1 — Arrival

  • Flight from Islamabad/Lahore/Karachi to Skardu
  • Check-in at hotel, rest & acclimatization
  • Evening walk by the river or bazaar visit

Day 2 — Skardu City Tour

  • Skardu Bazaar exploration
  • Upper Kachura Lake visit
  • Shangrila Resort
  • Bonfire night (optional)

Day 3 — Shigar Valley

  • Shigar Fort (heritage site)
  • Blind Lake
  • Sarfaranga Cold Desert
  • Return to Skardu in the evening

Day 4 — Khaplu Valley

  • Drive to Khaplu (3-4 hrs)
  • Visit Khaplu Fort & Chaqchan Mosque
  • Hike along the Hushe River
  • Overnight in Khaplu or return to Skardu

Day 5 — Departure

  • Breakfast & checkout
  • Return journey via road or flight
  • Stops at viewpoints & riverside areas
