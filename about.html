Our Story

Welcome to Western GB Travel & Tours

Western GB Travel & Tours is a leading travel company based in Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. We specialize in providing unforgettable travel experiences across the breathtaking Northern Areas of Pakistan.

From the majestic peaks of Skardu to the serene valleys of Hunza, from the lush meadows of Deosai to the ancient forts of Shigar — we bring you closer to the untouched beauty of Pakistan's north.

Whether you're a solo adventurer, a couple seeking romance, a family looking for memories, or a corporate group needing a retreat — we have the perfect package for you.
