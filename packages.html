Day by Day

Skardu 7-Day Itinerary

Our most detailed trip — every day is an adventure

Day 1 — Arrival in Skardu

  • Departure from Islamabad/Lahore/Karachi by Air or via Karakoram Highway
  • Arrival in Skardu, check-in at hotel
  • Rest & acclimatization
  • Evening walk by the river or bazaar visit

Day 2 — Skardu City Tour

  • Skardu Bazaar
  • Upper Kachura Lake
  • Shangrila Resort (Lower Kachura Lake)
  • Bonfire night (optional)

Day 3 — Shigar Valley Exploration

  • Shigar Fort (heritage museum)
  • Blind Lake
  • Sarfaranga Cold Desert
  • Return to Skardu in the evening

Day 4 — Khaplu Valley

  • Drive to Khaplu (approx. 3-4 hours)
  • Khaplu Fort
  • Chaqchan Mosque
  • Hike along the Hushe River
  • Overnight stay in Khaplu or return to Skardu

Day 5 — Deosai National Park (Summer)

  • Full day trip to Deosai Plains (2nd highest plateau in the world)
  • Sheosar Lake
  • Bara Pani
  • Wildlife spotting (marmots, possibly brown bears)
  • Return by evening

Day 6 — Waterfalls & Leisure

  • Mantholha Waterfall
  • Sermik Waterfall
  • Photography day
  • Optional hike or chill day at hotel

Day 7 — Departure

  • Breakfast & checkout
  • Return journey to Islamabad/Lahore via road or flight
  • Stops at viewpoints & riverside areas on the way back
